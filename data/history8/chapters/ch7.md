##### Third Anglo-Maratha War (1817-18)

In spite of signing the Subsidiary Alliance, Peshwa Baji Rao II wanted to free himself from British control. Thus, the Third Anglo-Maratha War started in 1817 when the Peshwa, with the support of the Holkars and Bhonsles, attacked the British Residency at Kirkee (now Khadki in Maharashtra). However, the Maratha forces were completely defeated by the British.
